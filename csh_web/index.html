Manufacturing Spices

At Samagi Spices, we believe in using only the finest ingredients to create our premium spices. Our manufacturing process starts with sourcing high-quality spices from the best regions, followed by expertly roasting, grinding, and blending to create unique, bold flavors for all of your culinary adventures.

01. Collection & Sorting

Only the best spices are selected and sorted.

02. Peeling & Rolling

Our spices are carefully peeled and rolled to release their unique flavors.

03. Fumigation

We take care of your health and fumigate to eliminate any potential pests.

04. Moisture Checking

Our spices are checked for moisture levels to ensure their freshness.

05. Grading

Our spices are graded to ensure that only the best quality spices make it to your kitchen.

06. Packing

Our spices are packed with care to ensure their freshness reaches you intact.

07. Adding a Final Touch

Our expert team gives a final touch to each spice to make sure you get the best flavor experience.

What is Sri Lankan cinnamon and why is it special? +
Sri Lankan cinnamon, also known as Ceylon cinnamon, is the highest quality variety of cinnamon, only grown in Sri Lanka. It has a sweet and delicate flavor, making it highly sought after in cooking and baking.
How is cinnamon harvested? +
Cinnamon is harvested from the inner bark of the cinnamon tree. The outer bark is stripped away, and the inner bark curls into quills as it dries.
